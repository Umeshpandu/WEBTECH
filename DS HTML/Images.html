Azhimala is a Shiva temple located on the coast of the Arabian Sea near Vizhinjam in the Thiruvananthapuram district of Kerala, India.

The temple is dedicated to Shiva and is known for the 18 m (58 ft) tall Gangadhareshwara sculpture, which is the tallest Shiva sculpture in Kerala.
